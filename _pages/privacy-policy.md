This is a personal portfolio website. I aim to keep data collection minimal, purposeful, and transparent.

## What this site collects

- Standard hosting logs may be processed by GitHub Pages or the hosting provider for security and reliability.
- This site uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage such as page visits, general traffic patterns, device/browser categories, and similar analytics signals.
- If you email me, I receive the information you choose to include in that email.

## What this site does not do

- It does not sell visitor information.
- It does not ask visitors to submit sensitive personal data.
- It does not provide a public contact form or publish private messages.
- It does not use analytics to make automated decisions about visitors.

## External links

This site links to GitHub, LinkedIn, X, and other external resources. Those services are outside this website and have their own privacy practices.
