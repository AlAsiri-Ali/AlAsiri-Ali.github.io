## Who I am

I am **Ali AlAsiri**, an AI and Information Systems professional from Saudi Arabia. My background started with a **Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems** from **King Khalid University**, which shaped the way I think about data, users, processes, software, and organizational workflows.

I later completed a **Professional Master in Artificial Intelligence** at **King Abdulaziz University** with an **Excellent** grade and a **4.98 GPA**. My current focus is building intelligent systems that are useful, measurable, and grounded in clear problem definitions.

## Focus areas

- **Intelligent systems:** connecting models, data, interfaces, and real-world use cases.
- **Machine learning and evaluation:** choosing suitable methods, comparing results, and interpreting outcomes responsibly.
- **Data science and decision modeling:** preparing data, building useful features, and translating analysis into decisions.
- **Generative workflows:** designing controlled AI workflows where outputs can be reviewed, compared, and improved.
- **Responsible implementation:** understanding practical limits, privacy considerations, and the context where a system will be used.

## The standard I aim for

A strong AI project is not only a model. It needs clean data, careful evaluation, privacy awareness, and a clear explanation of what worked, what did not, and what should be improved next.
